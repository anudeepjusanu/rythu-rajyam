Correspondent and Founders:
V Srinivas was born at Ujpuram EG Dist (AP) on 6th April 1974 is a son of V Venkata Rao who is a proud farmer. He studied upto intermediate MPC in Kakinada. Later he joined Indian Navy as a sailor on 19th Jan 1993 and has completed his maximum period of 15 years as a sailor and retired from Indian Navy on 30th Jan 2008. After that he has decided to serve for below poverty line, ex servicemen, Farmers
After 11 years he decided to start Rythu Rajyam (a charitable trust for below poverty line, ex servicemen and farmers) with small amount of investment and small teams


V Saroja Devi was born at pithapuram EG Dist (AP) on 02nd December 1977 is a daughter of Sri Akula Surya Narayana. She studied upto graduation(Bcom) in Kakinada.She is married to Srinivas on 22nd Feb 2000. She decided to Join hands with her husband as a correspondent and founder of Rythu Rajyam(charitable trust)

Vision and Mission

Mission of trust is support financially without any interest and to provide services to farmers like cultivation, Fertilizers, pesticides and provision of procurement and supply of all the farmer products(Food Grains, vegetables) from the farmers and to the farmers

Make the farmer happy with all facilities for cultivation. Rythu Rajyam trust should be one of the fastest growing farmers organization where as all the farmers in AP will make the Rythu Rajyam as a unique
